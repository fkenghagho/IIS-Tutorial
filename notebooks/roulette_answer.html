IID Assumption: Independent Identically Distributed Events

The crucial assumption in standard probability models for gambling is that each spin of the roulette wheel is a statistically independent event. This means the outcome of any previous spin (e.g., the 50 consecutive red results) has absolutely no physical or mathematical influence on the outcome of the next spin (spin 51).
